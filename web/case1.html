Case Study: 1

So this is a simple website like indeed and Glassdoor. Nothing much fancy.

Feaure of that website:

a. Any company can register their and post jobs for their organization.

b. User can go to that page and upload therir docs and then any admin of that organiztion forward or reject their application

I register myself as a org. and after that i fill out things like- job title, min exp, salary, and bla bla bla. But I didn't arrpove my that job- Status: Pending.

Then I just view that job and then I saw some numeric id.

I send that request to repeater i tried to change that id[random number] to view other's info but pretty good luck. I got 403 forbidden.

I just changed my request from GET to DELETE and boom!!.

And I got 200 OK

Then after I create a new account and then copy my new account job-id to to my and replace it with my first account job-id.

And yes, from this attacker can bruteforce that job-id and delete nearly whole data.

In API, always rate limit is also important.
